Session 5: Repeated Second-Price Sealed Bid Auction

You will be randomly paired with one participant at the beginning of this session and remain paired with them for all 10 rounds.

Each round, you will receive a valuation between 0.01 and 100.00. You will submit a bid without knowing your opponent’s valuation or bid.

Payoff Rules:

  • If your bid is lower than your opponent’s, you earn 0 points.
  • If your bid is higher, you earn: valuation − opponent’s bid.
  • If bids are equal, you earn: (valuation − opponent’s bid) / 2.

You have 1 minute to submit your bid each round. If you do not submit a bid in time, you will earn 0 points.
