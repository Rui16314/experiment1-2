Session 3: First-Price Sealed Bid Auction with Communication

You will be randomly paired with a different participant each round.

Each round, you will receive a valuation between 0.01 and 1.00. You will submit a bid without knowing your opponent’s valuation.

New Feature: You may communicate with your opponent before submitting your bid. Please keep your identity confidential.

Payoff Rules:

  • If your bid is lower than your opponent’s, you earn 0 points.
  • If your bid is higher, you earn: valuation − bid.
  • If bids are equal, you earn: (valuation − bid) / 2.

You have 1 minute to submit your bid each round. If you do not submit a bid in time, you will earn 0 points.
