Welcome to Session 1: First-Price Sealed Bid Auction

You will be randomly paired with another participant each round.

Your valuation will be between 0.00 and 1.00. You will submit a bid without knowing your opponent’s valuation or bid.

If your bid is higher, you win and earn: valuation − bid.

If bids are equal, payoff is split: (valuation − bid) / 2.

If your bid is lower, you earn 0 points.
